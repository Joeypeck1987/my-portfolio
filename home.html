About

I am learning to turn scattered curiosity into working structures: code, design, writing, music, art, and discipline that survives ordinary days. I like projects with a practical face and a strange pulse underneath.

“A page can be plain and still carry weather. A skill can begin clumsy and still become a craft.”
What I am building toward

A life where technical skill and creative nerve are not separate rooms. I want to understand systems, make useful things, and keep the work alive enough to avoid becoming mechanical.
